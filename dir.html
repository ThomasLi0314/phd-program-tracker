People

Members of the Oden Institute community are involved in a wide range of programs and research initiatives. Learn more about those that make up our community in their profiles. Use the tools below to search by role, affiliation, research interest area, and more.

Glossary of terms

  • Core Faculty: a subset of the Oden Institute Affiliated Faculty who maintain a high level of activity in the Institute (research projects, committees, CSEM PhD student dissertations, postdoctoral researchers, teaching, organizing seminars and workshops).
  • CSEM Student: Graduate student enrolled in the CSEM Program.
  • GSC Faculty: Member of the CSEM Graduate Studies Committee (GSC). GSC faculty may chair CSEM dissertation committees. GSC includes Oden Institute Core Faculty, some Affiliated faculty and Senior Oden Institute research staff approved by the Graduate School.
  • Student Leader: CSEM graduate student serving as a CSEM Student Representative or Graduate Student Assembly (GSA) Representative.
